MY FUTURE:

* In the future, I see myself as a successful and professional educator who is financially stable. I hope to have a house in the province for my parents and be able to afford both my needs and wants, especially attending concerts of my favorite boy groups and buying their merchandise. I also dream of traveling, whether alone or with friends and cousins, both locally and internationally. I wish for good health for myself and for my parents, aunt, uncle, and grandparents, and that they will still be with me for a long time. I also plan to have my own flower bouquet business or franchise one of my favorite food stores, such as Moshi Manju. Lastly, I hope to live a life full of happiness.

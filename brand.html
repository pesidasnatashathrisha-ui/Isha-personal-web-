MAHALIMA

BEAUTIFULLY CRAFTED FLOWERS

Crafted with love, inspired by five

The logo of my brand is called “Mahalima Flower Crafts” represents my passion for creating handmade satin and fuzzy wire flowers, which symbolize creativity, care, and beauty. The name “Mahalima” is inspired by SB19, a five-member boy group. It combines “mahal” (love) and “lima” (five), showing my admiration for them and the strong connection between SB19 and their fans, A’TIN.

The colors also have meaning. Blue represents peace and calmness, while green symbolizes nature, reflecting my love for natural beauty. In all, the logo expresses my creativity, inspiration, and the love behind my craft.
